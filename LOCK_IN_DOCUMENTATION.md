**App Name:** LOCK IN  
**Tagline:** The productivity engine for the chronically online. Weaponize your ADHD. Gamify the grind.  
**Core Concept:** A Gen Z-focused, AI-powered "Command Center" that turns daily tasks into a high-stakes video game HUD. It replaces boring calendars with "Quests," "Boss Battles," and "Aura Points."

## üéØ The Problem (Slide 1)
*   **Traditional Planners are Boring:** Spreadsheets and standard calendars feel like chores.
*   **Analysis Paralysis:** Users have too many tasks and don't know where to start.
*   **Burnout & Doomscrolling:** Gen Z struggles with switching between "Rotting" (doing nothing) and "Grinding."
*   **Lack of Immediate Reward:** Completing tasks feels unrewarding in standard apps.

## ‚ö° The Solution: LOCK IN (Slide 2)
*   **AI-Driven:** Uses Google Gemini 2.5 to automatically structure the day based on energy levels and chronotype.
*   **Gamified:** Every task is a quest. Focus time is a "Boss Battle." Streaks and XP ("Aura") drive engagement.
*   **Aesthetic First:** 15+ themes (Cyber, Y2K, Vaporwave) make the app feel like a personalized OS.
*   **Honest AI:** Features "Roast Mode" to critique bad habits and "Rot Mode" to acknowledge burnout healthily.

## üõ†Ô∏è Key Features Breakdown (for Demo Slides)

### 1. The Yap Pad (Input Protocol)
*   **What it is:** An AI-powered brain dump area.
*   **How it works:** Users type unstructured thoughts (e.g., "I need to study math and maybe hit the gym and buy milk").
*   **The AI Magic:** The system analyzes the "Yap," extracts actionable tasks, assigns priority, and adds them to the quest log automatically.

### 2. Context-Aware Scheduling
*   **Vibe Check Widget:** Users select their current energy (e.g., "Full Send," "Zen Mode," "Cooked").
*   **Smart Allocation:** The AI generates a schedule that respects the user's "Vibe."
    *   *High Energy:* Stacks difficult tasks back-to-back.
    *   *Low Energy:* Inserts "Touch Grass" breaks and focuses on easy wins.

### 3. Boss Mode (Focus Execution)
*   **What it is:** A full-screen, immersive focus timer.
*   **Visuals:** Distractions are removed. A giant timer dominates the screen. Background pulses with the user's heartbeat/intensity.
*   **Gamification:** Completing a session logs "Time Spent" and awards massive Aura points.

### 4. Roast My Schedule (Gen Z Feature)
*   **Concept:** Instead of toxic positivity, the AI offers "Emotional Damage."
*   **Function:** It analyzes the user's planned day and critiques it using slang (e.g., "You have 4 hours of breaks? You're cooked.").
*   **Goal:** Uses humor to shame the user into productivity.

### 5. Rot Mode (Wellness Protocol)
*   **Concept:** Validating the need to do nothing.
*   **Visual Change:** The entire UI turns grayscale and dims.
*   **Function:** Encourages rest without guilt. System messages change to "It's okay to do nothing."

### 6. Digital Receipts (Social Proof)
*   **Viral Feature:** Generates an aesthetic, shopping-style receipt of the day's completed tasks.
*   **Use Case:** Users download this image to share on Instagram Stories/Snapchat to prove they were productive.

## üé® Design & Aesthetics (Slide: "The Vibe")

LOCK IN features a robust theming engine. Users can switch their "Operating System" instantly to match their current vibe.

### Theme Roster (15 Presets)
1.  **Cyber (Default):** Dark mode with neon purple/violet accents. Retro-futuristic arcade feel.
2.  **Y2K:** Matrix-inspired. Deep black backgrounds with hacker green text and terminal fonts.
3.  **Brat:** Aggressive, high-energy lime green. Chaotic and bold.
4.  **Cozy:** Lo-fi aesthetic. Warm coffee browns, stone grays, and pastel orange highlights.
5.  **Drift:** Synthwave/Outrun style. Midnight blue backgrounds with hot pink and electric blue.
6.  **Vapor:** Vaporwave aesthetic. Deep purple base with cyan and magenta gradients.
7.  **Acid:** High contrast. Pure black background with acid green and hot pink accents.
8.  **Goth:** Whimsigoth vibes. Dark violet, deep purple, and mystical elements.
9.  **Cloud:** Dreamy and soft. Indigo nights with soft purple and periwinkle.
10. **Retro:** 90s PC terminal. Dark gray/blue with amber monochrome text and red alerts.
11. **Glitch:** Glitchcore. Stark black with aggressive red and cyan offsets.
12. **Luxe:** "Old Money" aesthetic. Matte stone blacks with metallic gold and emerald accents.
13. **Void:** Ultra-minimalist. Pure black and white. No distractions.
14. **Sunset:** Golden hour vibes. Warm dark browns, rich oranges, and yellows.
15. **Mint:** Fresh and clean. Deep emerald greens with bright teal and mint highlights.

### UI Elements
*   **Glassmorphism:** Frosted glass panels for a modern look.
*   **Glitch Text:** Headers randomly glitch to fit the cybernetic theme.
*   **Holographic Gradients:** Backgrounds feature subtle, grainy noise and glowing orbs.
*   **Monospace Typography:** Uses 'Space Grotesk' and 'JetBrains Mono' for a technical, HUD-like appearance.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Tech Stack (Slide: "Under the Hood")
*   **Frontend Framework:** React 19 (Components, Hooks).
*   **Build Tool:** Vite (Fast hot-reloading).
*   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (Utility-first styling, dark mode).
*   **AI Intelligence:** Google Gemini API (`gemini-2.5-flash` for logic, `gemini-2.5-flash-image` for visuals).
*   **Data Visualization:** Recharts (Productivity spline charts).
*   **Persistence:** LocalStorage (Privacy-first, instant load).
*   **Export:** `html2canvas` for generating receipt images.
